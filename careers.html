WORKING AT MANDERLEY HOME

We believe in mentoring every employee and empowering individuals to grow their gifts within our firm. Collaboration over competition is our culture.

/APPLY HERE

If Kelly Wearstler and Shea McGee had a baby it would be you! You are truly a creative, a fashionista and have a fabulous eye for color, pattern and textures. You see tonal differences in paint and know how to pick colors in a room without having to look up what's popular on Pinterest.

You often find yourself rearranging furniture with creative placements. HGTV is your favorite channel and you dream of picking out tiles for bathrooms and fabulous kitchens. You are comfortable working in multiple design genres and not just "RH or Modern Farmhouse". You keep up with style trends and can speak to what is happening each design season.

Wait what? You love styling a bookshelf on the fly? Be still my heart.

Job Duties:
  • Up to 10 hours per week
  • Source furniture and soft goods for interior projects online as well as in person in showrooms.
  • Source tile and stone for remodel projects
  • Collaborate on concept designs, room layout with our creative director
  • Must be willing to drive to local showrooms to pickup/return samples
  • Create design board presentations in Canva and Flat Lays for client presentations
  • Must be able to lift 25 lbs.
Pay is commensurate with experience in the following:
  • Canva experience
  • Google Drive/sheets
  • Ability to do perspective hand drawings
  • Experience working with trade furniture lines
  • Experience working in construction and residential remodels
  • 2 years or more working in residential design
  • Specifying custom window treatments

We are seeking a highly motivated interior design intern to join our firm. This opportunity will offer valuable in-depth, hands-on experience within the industry. Applicants must reside near our office and be able to reliably commute to job sites in the east bay area.

This is a paid internship. To be considered for this opportunity, potential candidates must submit a cover letter demonstrating intent, a resume, and any portfolio photos.

Qualifications:
  • Strong verbal, written and graphic communication skills
  • Resourceful, self-motivated, enthusiastic team player
  • Desire to learn AutoCAD and Canva
Primary Responsibilities Include:
  • Varied administrative tasks such as accepting and managing deliveries and returns
  • Finish and product research and sourcing
  • Social Media Posting, photography, copy edits and video
  • Assist designer with presentation, mood boards and material palettes
  • Correspondence and coordination of vendors and showrooms
