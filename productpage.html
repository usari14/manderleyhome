GEORGIA LANE

You need an interior visionary you can trust with your whole home remodel. Custom home builds and remodels are complex undertakings with many moving parts. But you have a life full of children, travel, weddings, and work to balance!

We know the journey ahead, have seen what can go wrong, and know how to problem-solve quickly and effectively. This Tudor style home in Danville no longer feels dark and outdated. By listening to our client’s desire for a modern coastal style, and infusing our own tasteful aesthetic, we’ve created a home that feels approachable and completely delightful.

When I walk in a room all I see is potential and I am so happy we moved a few walls in this kitchen remodel. We designed a new coffee bar and a built in banquette with performance grade fabric seating. By completely revising the layout of the primary bathroom, we created a spa like sanctuary for luxurious bathing rituals. This is what it looks like, to step into interior design, with Manderley by your side.
