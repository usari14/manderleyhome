My job is to help you define your design style and take the execution off your hands.

THE PROCESS BREAKDOWN

01 / MEET AND GREET

We’ll start with a casual Zoom call to get to know each other! I’ll ask about your design style, your timeline, your budget, your biggest wishes and goals, and where you are in the construction process. Basically, the basics!

02 / SITE VISIT

If we seem like a good fit, we’ll set up a paid site visit so our team can see what we’re working with! I’ll also ask you to share a Pinterest board of design inspo so I can see your vision in action and make sure our styles align.

03 / BOOK & SIGN

Before you officially book, I’ll share a proposal with an itemized scope of work. Once you’ve approved that scope, you’ll receive a proposal with the cost breakdown. If all looks good, you’ll pay a retainer to secure your spot!

04 / LAUNCH

Your launch date is when your project really kicks off! We’ll complete a site measure, an inventory assessment, a budget, and a timeline. We’ll also have an all-hands meeting with your general contractor so everybody knows each other.

05 / CREATIVE DIRECTION

Finally, the real fun begins! And by real fun, I mean real decisions! We’ll dive deep into your photo inspiration, curate a mood board, and define the color palette of your new home. You’ll start to see a defined style & aesthetic quickly come to life.

06 / DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

Once we have the ideas down, it’s time to make them happen. We’ll select FFE (that’s designer talk for furniture, fixtures, and equipment — think tile, cabinetry, stone, lighting, and appliances) and do a final floor plan edit. The big picture stuff!

07 / PROCUREMENT & MANAGEMENT

Procurement (also known as Chaos Management) is when we purchase everything you’ve chosen. We’ll coordinate deliveries, communicate with your vendors, and oversee design implementation to ensure it all goes smoothly!

08 / INSTALLATION & FINAL REVEAL

The last step is installation. We assemble and arrange your furniture, roll out your rugs, and hang your lighting fixtures, window treatments, and decor. You’ll walk in on reveal day ready to start life in your brand new home. Swoon away.
