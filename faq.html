Most projects take about 4-9 months to complete from first meeting to final delivery and styling. Occasionally, manufacturing delays combined with high demand for interior furniture can lead to long lead times and a lack of inventory. We highly encourage you to calculate potential delays in your project timeline.

We’ll do everything in our power to get your project completed in the fastest timeframe. What we won’t compromise on is quality. Pressure, birthdays, holidays, parties, and guests will unfortunately not speed up the process. We ask that you respect the timeline and avoid rushing the team as it won’t speed up the schedule. Design, selection, administration, purchasing, receiving, and quality control of materials such as paint, tile, flooring, stone facades, slab countertops, cabinetry, appliances, plumbing, lighting, and any other details specific to your project.

Since every project is as unique as every client, the services needed and the hours required to complete them will vary widely. Therefore we bid each project individually. Our bid is comprised of a flat rate for design services and hourly packages for procurement and construction administration.
